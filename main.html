My Life in Nandaluru

The Village That Raised Me

Nandaluru isn’t on the tourist map. But for us, it’s home. A place where mornings start with temple bells, and strangers don’t exist.

I’ve grown here, failed here, loved here. My identity isn’t something I built outside — I built it right here on dusty roads and sunburnt skin.
